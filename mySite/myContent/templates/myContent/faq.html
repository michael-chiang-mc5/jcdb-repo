Frequently asked questions

What is JournalClubDB?
JournalClubDB is an online forum where you can upload and discuss scientific articles

JournalClubDB is NOT a long term storage solution for your papers. We may delete your old papers (uploaded >3 months ago) if we run out of server space.
When I log in with social media, google/facebook/twitter tells me that JournalClubDB can view my email address, etc. What are you doing with my information?
JournalClubDB is committed to protecting your privacy, and we do absolutely nothing with your information. We selected the most minimally intrusive settings when setting up social authentication with google/facebook/twitter.
What are the rules at JCDB?
Don't do anything illegal. This includes obeying copyright law. Note the articles you upload can only be read by your group so you should be covered by Fair Use laws as long as you do not broadcast your secret key to the whole world.

Click here.
How can I get in contact with the developers?
Click here. We're pretty responsive to emails.
Will JournalClubDB become pay-to-use in the future?
JCDB is free-to-use and it will remain free.
Who can access the articles that I upload?
JCDB is designed so that only people who you invite into your group can see the articles you upload. Full disclosure, JCDB is not hardened against attack; please do not upload your top-secret Benghazi emails to our servers.
